OG

THE INTERNATIONAL CHAMPIONS
OG Dota Pro Team consists of five main player: Ceb, Topson, Notail, Jarex, and Ana

Here's how OG become a two-time TI champion

  • Notail and Fly started the Dota 2 organization: OG
  • Fly leave OG a month before TI8
  • Notail heart was broken, but he didn't give up.
  • He and Ceb re-organize the team from ground up.
  • They found Topson, a pub star, and Ana, a man with few words.
  • They had to play from the TI8 qualifier since they don't have enough DPC point.
  • They made it to the TI8 and finally they won the agies.
  • Many said it was a flute or a one time luck.
  • They came back stronger at TI9.
  • Ana IO was broken and feared by all teams.
  • Final match of TI9 was Liquid VS OG
  • They won the agies with 3-1 result.
  • Now no one can say OG is a flute.
  • They are two-time back-to-back TI champion.
